Music Inspo Generator

An Application Made By Naiya Roe

Instructions: This app is for all of those people who are in music funks and can't think of what music to listen to. This app is a simple way to get a few suggestions by going under each of the genres and clicking the button. Once the button is clicked, a song suggeston comes up! Each song will have a link to where you can listen to it on Spotify. There is also a space where you can add your own songs for when you find some but don't want to rememebr it. Have fun!
