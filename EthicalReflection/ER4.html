Ethical Reflection#4 -Automation and the Economy

As humans became lazier to do things for ourselves, we started to build technology that would do it for us. Ever since the beginning of time, we built tools that made our lives easier. There are tractors for agriculture, cars for transportation, and kitchen gadgets to help cook our food. Then we started to have a newer kind of technology using computers. We have smartphones, laptops, and tablets that, in some ways, made our lives easier. As technology kept advancing, we now have actual robots that can perform human functions ranging from pouring a cup of coffee to analyzing CT scans to check for cancer. If these automations become smarter, it is easy to think that they will soon replace our jobs. So will it?
